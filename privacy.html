1. INTRODUCTION

PixiTales (“we,” “our,” or “us”) provides an AI-powered storybook generation platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use your information when you register and use our services. We comply with global data protection standards, including the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (PDPO) of Hong Kong, as well as relevant principles of GDPR, COPPA, and APPI.

2. DATA WE COLLECT

To provide our services, we only collect the following minimal information:

  • Account Information: Email address and username provided during registration.
  • User-Generated Content: Text prompts and AI-generated stories/images saved to your account.
  • Technical Data: IP address and device identifiers for security and performance monitoring.

3. AGE REQUIREMENTS & PARENTAL CONSENT

While our App does not specifically request the age of its users, our content is designed for families.

  • Under 18: If you are under the age of 18 (or the age of majority in your jurisdiction), you must only use this App with the involvement and consent of a parent or legal guardian.
  • Parental Control: We encourage parents to supervise their children's digital activities. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child without parental consent, we will delete that information immediately.

You have the right to access, update, or delete your account information at any time through the App settings. In accordance with the Hong Kong PDPO and other regional laws, you may also request a copy of your data or withdrawal of consent by contacting us.
